About Foodback

Foodback is a new initiative to empower people for healthier community food places. It involves the development of a smartphone app for crowdsourcing data on the healthiness of foods advertised and sold in local community settings (i.e., schools, hospitals, supermarkets, takeaways, sport clubs) and outdoors (i.e., around schools). The results will be fed back automatically to local ‘change agents’ (such as local Government representatives, local Members of Parliament, schools, retailers, sport clubs,..) in the form of health star ratings. This way, settings and communities can be compared against each other, and people can encourage and support local ‘change agents’ to make positive, healthy changes to foods advertised and sold in their community. Registration information will include: sex, age, ethnicity, community, email, and mobile phone number. Your location based on your mobile device will also be captured. You will also be required to use your mobile device’s camera for this application.
